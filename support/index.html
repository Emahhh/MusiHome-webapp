Frequently Asked Questions

What music services does the app support?

Our app supports both Spotify and Apple Music.

Do I need an Apple Music or Spotify account to listen to the music?

Yes, you need an active Apple Music or Spotify account to listen to music. MusiHome provides a convenient shortcut to open a Spotify or Apple Music resource, but it does not host any music itself.

Does the app work offline?

You need an internet connection to create a shortcut. Once the shortcut is created, it can work offline under the following conditions:

  • If you have Spotify, the app works fine offline.
  • If you have Apple Music, due to Apple Music's limitations, shortcuts can only be opened when online.
